FAQ
What is Los Santos County RP?

It is a unique project based on GTA, allowing thousands of players to interact with each other in real-time.

What RP (RolePlay) stands for?

This is a game mode where players create characters and scenarios, and then act according to their roles within the gameplay.

How do I start on LSC RP?

First you need to download a licensed GTA V and then install the Majestic Launcher.

What to do on the server?

In addition to a large choice of jobs and player interactions, we have regular updates and seasonal events available.

Where can I find the server rules?

You can find a section with server rules, complaints about players and other topics on the project forum.

Need assistance?

Our friendly community is always ready to lend a helping hand. Join discussions, seek guidance from seasoned players.
